About Me

Hi! I’m Helen, and I’m enthusiastic about many things. The first among them is our planet – I firmly believe that the best way to save it is through science and engineering, which is why I have devoted much of my time towards research of sustainable technologies, such as reducing carbon dioxide into useful products, and creating higher capacity batteries to power the population renewably.

I love being creative. I have been programming since high school and enjoy using the tools it provides to enhance the lives of myself and others. I also enjoy working with CAD software to design new systems to solve engineering problems.

In my spare time, I volunteer as Vice President Finance for the Waterloo Engineering Society, where I make sure the Engineering student body is supported and help advise Inclusivity-related initiatives. I also love writing, reading, knitting, and making music.
